▶ Resume transfers

Never lose a transfer again

Bad Wi-Fi? Battery died? Connection dropped? No problem. Sharekit automatically saves your upload progress. When you come back, your transfer picks up exactly where it left off — no need to start from the beginning.

  • Automatic progress saving — no manual action needed
  • Resumes from the exact byte where you left off
  • Works even if you close the browser and come back
  • Shows clear progress for each file being uploaded
  • Perfect for large video files on slow connections

🔒 Password protection

Lock your transfers with a password

Add an extra layer of security to your share links. With Pro and Unlimited plans, you can protect any transfer with a password. Only people who know the password can download your files — even if someone else gets the link.

  • Set any password you want for each transfer
  • Recipients are shown a password entry screen
  • Wrong password = no access, no download
  • Passwords are encrypted using SHA-256 — never stored in plain text
  • Available on Pro and Unlimited plans

⏱ Link expiry

Control how long your links last

Sharekit links don't live forever unless you want them to. Choose how long your share link stays active — from 3 days on the free plan, up to 30 days on Pro, or never-expiring on Unlimited. After expiry, files are permanently deleted from our servers.

  • Free plan: 3-day link expiry
  • Pro plan: Up to 30-day link expiry
  • Unlimited plan: Links never expire
  • Clear countdown shown to recipient — they know when to download
  • Files permanently deleted on expiry — better for privacy

🔐 Security

Your privacy is always protected

Sharekit is built with security at its core. Every file you upload is protected with industry-standard encryption. We automatically scan for viruses, we never sell your data, and we never show ads inside our product.

  • Files encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest

  • No ad tracking or third-party data sharing
  • Files permanently deleted after expiry date
  • GDPR-compliant data handling practices
