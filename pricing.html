Common questions

Pricing FAQ

Do I need a credit card for the free plan?▾
No credit card required. Sign up with just your email address and start sharing immediately for free.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?▾
Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Pro or Unlimited at any time. To downgrade, contact support@sharekit.in and we'll process it at the end of your billing period.
Does the yearly plan auto-renew?▾
Cashfree payment forms are one-time payments — they don't auto-renew automatically. You will need to re-purchase when your plan expires. We'll send you a reminder email before expiry.
What happens to my files if I downgrade?▾
Your existing transfers and share links remain active until their expiry date. After downgrading, new transfers will follow the Free plan limits. We will not delete existing uploaded files.
Is there a GST invoice for Pro/Unlimited plans?▾
Yes. After payment, Cashfree provides a payment receipt. For a GST invoice, email support@sharekit.in with your payment details and GSTIN and we'll issue one within 2 business days.
