About Sharekit

Built in India, for everyone

Sharekit started with one simple question: why is sharing a file still so complicated in 2025? We built the answer.

Our story

Sharing files should be as simple as handing something to a friend. But for most people, it means struggling with USB cables, WhatsApp compression destroying video quality, email attachment limits, or confusing apps that require both people to install something.

Sharekit was built to fix this. A lightweight, fast, secure web app that works on literally any device with a browser. No app to install, no account required to receive files, no complicated setup. Just drag, drop, and share.

We're a small, independent team based in India, building tools that work for real people — students sending assignments, professionals sharing large presentations, families sharing holiday photos, and businesses transferring critical documents.

Our mission

To make file sharing so simple and private that anyone — from a 60-year-old parent to a professional developer — can use it without a tutorial.

We believe your files are your business. We don't read them, we don't sell your data, and we don't show you ads. Our only business model is a fair subscription for users who need more storage and more features. That's it.
